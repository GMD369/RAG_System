Chapter 03

Crafting a Grounded Answer

Retrieved chunks alone don't give you an answer — they give you raw text. Answer generation stitches the question and context together into a carefully engineered prompt, then sends it to a large language model. The model is instructed to answer only from the provided context and cite its sources.
