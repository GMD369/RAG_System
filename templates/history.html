Chapter 04

Having a Real Conversation

Basic RAG treats every question as independent. But real conversations have context — "Who succeeded him?" only makes sense if you know who "him" refers to. History-aware RAG detects ambiguous follow-ups, rewrites them into self-contained queries, then retrieves and answers using the full conversation context.
