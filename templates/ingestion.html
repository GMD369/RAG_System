Chapter 01

Teaching the AI Your Documents

Before the AI can answer anything, it must read your documents. But language models can't search through raw files — they think in numbers. The ingestion pipeline converts every page into a list of floating-point vectors that capture meaning, then stores them so they can be searched in milliseconds.

Why overlap=150?

Chunk A …tail text…
← 150 chars shared →
…tail text… Chunk B

Without overlap, a sentence that straddles two chunks would be split in half — the AI sees only a fragment and misses the answer. Overlapping ensures every sentence appears whole in at least one chunk.
