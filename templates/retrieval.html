Chapter 02

Finding the Right Information

You ask a question. The system doesn't search for keywords — it converts your question into the same kind of vector it used for documents, then finds the chunks whose vectors are mathematically closest (cosine similarity). The top results become the AI's context window.

What is Cosine Similarity?

Two vectors point in similar directions when they share meaning. Cosine similarity measures the angle between them — 1.0 means identical direction (perfect match), 0.0 means completely unrelated. We only return chunks scoring ≥ 0.3 to avoid noise.
